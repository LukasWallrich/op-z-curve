  • CMBE
    • Certified Management & Business Educator

      • The CMBE gives business and management educators a recognised professional designation and a framework for continuous professional development. The scheme supports educators to further develop their teaching practice and helps to advance the quality of business and management education in higher education.

        Read more

    • Why become a CMBE?

      The CMBE is your commitment to advancing the quality of learning and teaching in the fields of business and management education.

    • Benefits for business schools

      The CMBE advances the development of business and management educators on an ongoing basis through a commitment to continuous professional development.

    • Eligibility & application

      The scheme is for those who teach, or lead learning and teaching activities, in the fields of business and management.
